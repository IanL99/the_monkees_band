ABOUT US:

The theme from our tv show begins as below:
"Hey, hey, we're the Monkees
And people say we monkey around
But we're to busy singing
To put anybody down"
And we've been singing ever since.

The Monkees was originally an American situation comedy that aired on NBC from September 12, 1966 to March 25, 1968.
The series follows the adventures of four young men (US), trying to make a name for ourselves as a rock 'n roll band.
Originally (and for many years), we were composed of Americans Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, and Peter Tork; and British actor and singer Davy Jones,
though sadly Davy Jones died in 2012.
We have sold more than 75 million records and sold more records than The Beatles and The Rolling Stones combined in 1967.
Our most famous songs include "Last Train to Clarksville", "Pleasant Valley Sunday", "Daydream Believer", and "I'm a Believer".

On this website you have access to group memorabilia including band member photos, some of our popular songs and a video of Daydream Believer.
Any new material will be added to this website as it becomes available. You can also visit Facebook/Twitter or Youtube to keep up to date choose links above.
